Biography

I am a year-4 undergraduate at Sichuan University and I am about to finish my bachelar career in June 2023. Currently, I am working with Prof. Xiaomeng Li, who is also my future PhD supervisor. I am about to start pursuing my PhD degree at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in August 2023. Previously, I am fortune to work with Prof. Yan Wang at Sichuan University as a Research Assistant.

My research lies at advancing medical image analysis with AI, with recent focus on learning from weak or inabundant annotations. In particular, I am interested in weakly/semi supervised image segmentation/classification.
